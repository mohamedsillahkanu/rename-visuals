Bulk Operations Explained
🔍
FIND & REPLACE

Instantly replace a text pattern across ALL values in a selected column. Perfect for removing prefixes, fixing abbreviations, or standardising codes.

BULK FIND & REPLACE
dist_ → (empty) Replace All
Example: Find dist_ → Replace with (blank) converts dist_Bombali into Bombali across all rows instantly.
🔤
TEXT TRANSFORMATIONS

Apply formatting rules to all values at once. Standardise inconsistent casing without typing each replacement manually.

AVAILABLE TRANSFORMATIONS
UPPERCASE western urban → WESTERN URBAN
lowercase BOMBALI → bombali
Title Case bo district → Bo District
Capitalize kenema → Kenema
